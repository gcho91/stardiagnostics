About Star Diagnostics

Mission

Star Diagnostics' mission is to create and deliver top quality diagnostic solutions for patients with symptoms of neuromuscular abnormalities (nerve damage) using state-of-the-art equipment and superior customer service.

Purpose

Our purpose, using integrity and strength, is to serve the community by providing great customer service and high technology services for the diagnosing and treatment planning of individuals with nerve and muscle conditions.

Technology

Star Diagnostics utilizes cutting-edge technology to provide the highest level of care & quality in the industry.

Mobile Testing Facility

Star Diagnostics is a mobile diagnostic company providing state of the art neuro-diagnostic testing. Certified by Medicare as a mobile Independent Diagnostics Testing Facility has allowed us to be open in multiple locations to better serve individuals in the metropolitan and underserved areas.

Our professional and friendly staff only performs EMG’s and Nerve Studies therefore have more experience and knowledge in the field of Electrodiagnositc's than most medical groups. We are here to answer any questions or concerns and welcome your feedback.

Our current locations are based in Texas and include the cities of Dallas, Fort Worth, Ennis, Canton, Tyler, and San Antonio.

Bridging the Gap

Star Diagnostics is bringing together people, medicine, technology, and treatment options by providing the best current services available in an accurate, fast, and efficient manner.



Ease of Access

Star Diagnostics has put a lot of effort and energy into providing ease of access for Physicians to send orders/prescriptions through our online web-portal to expedite the processing and insurance verification to ensure patients get scheduled as soon as possible (we strive to have individuals scheduled within 72 hours).

Online Reports - Real Time Reports

With all Physicians that have registered with Star Diagnostics web-portal, report of findings of the testing is available from the web as soon as the results have been interpreted. This means your physician can access reports from the web or their mobile device at any location. If your physician has multiple locations and does not have the ability to get to their fax machine, they can simply download or review your report so your treatment plan can be confirmed or altered.
